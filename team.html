Mission

Field is creating the new standard for sports recruitment, at the tip of your fingers. We are using technology to create the most simple, fun, and immersive experiences while solving many of the problems young athletes and recruiters around the world face today.

Vision

We are here to help athletes achieve their dreams, live their passions, improve their skills, grow their potential, and have equal opportunity all over the world. We want to allow athletes to live their lives to the fullest, and not have any regrets.
